D40g

2-in-1 Blood Pressure and Blood Glucose Monitoring System

Remotely monitor and adjust treatment plans to keep chronic diseases under control. Patients can easily monitor their blood glucose levels and blood pressure by themselves or with the help of a caregiver at any place, anytime–in an instant. Information can be transmitted to the FORA® 24/7 HealthView Telehealth System via the built-in cellular connectivity in the device.

  • 2-in-1 blood glucose and blood pressure measurements
  • Audible results in English/Spanish
  • Alternative site testing approved in blood glucose measurement
  • Smart Averaging Technology (AVG Mode)
  • Irregular Rapid Beat (IRB) technology
  • Internal 4G network SIM card

FORA Cellular Gateway
Multi-Parameter Data Transmission

Easily Connects to the Cloud

Works with Multiple Devices

Cellular or Bluetooth Connectivity

The Telehealth Gateway enables users to upload measurement data from any meter to the FORA cloud-based telehealth system. By connecting via Bluetooth to the measuring device, the Gateway allows for easy and seamless transmission.

Clinicians, patients, and family members alike are then able to view and track the patient’s results and overall status via the FORA 24/7 HealthView Telehealth System.

  • Easy operation: just plug it into the wall
  • Data upload to FORA 24/7 HealthView
  • Internal 4G network SIM card
  • Supports multi-parameter FORA devices with Bluetooth technology

FORA 24/7 HealthView Telehealth Cloud

Using wireless technology, the HIPAA-compliant 24/7 HealthView system provides tools that empower healthcare professionals to remotely monitor patient conditions—effectively leading to better outcomes.
